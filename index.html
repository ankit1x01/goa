Farewell Goa

Day 1: Bohemian Rhapsody in Arambol

Arrive at Mopa Airport (GOX) and glide into the bohemian heart of North Goa. We'll explore the serene Sweet Water Lake before finding our rhythm at the famous Arambol sunset drum circle.

Evening Session: The Art of Arrival

A gentle icebreaker and intention-setting circle to shed our city skins and fully arrive in the present moment, connecting with the land and each other.

Day 2: Forts, Fleas & Freedom

Channel your inner explorer as we visit the iconic Chapora 'Dil Chahta Hai' Fort and the stunningly restored Reis Magos Fort. Dive into the vibrant chaos of the Anjuna Flea Market and end the day at the lively Baga Beach.

Day 3: South Goa's Secret Shores

We journey south to tranquility. A boat will ferry us to the hidden Butterfly Beach for a magical experience. Later, we'll chill on the crescent shores of Palolem Beach, perfect for kayaking or simply being.

Sunset Session: The Freedom of Forgiveness

A profound act of self-love. As the sun melts into the sea, we'll guide you through a session of releasing old anchors and emotional baggage.

Day 4: The Majestic Dudhsagar Falls

An entire day dedicated to an epic adventure! We'll trek through the lush Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary to witness the awe-inspiring Dudhsagar Waterfalls, the 'Sea of Milk'. This isn't just a trek; it's a journey inward, overcoming challenges together.

Day 5: A Slice of Goan Culture

Immerse in culture! We'll wander through the colourful lanes of Fontainhas, Panjim's Latin Quarter. Then, a visit to a Spice Plantation for an aromatic tour and a traditional Goan lunch.

Farewell Bonfire: Integration & Storytelling

Under the stars, we share stories, laughter, and reflections from our journey, integrating our experiences so we can carry the magic of Goa home with us.

Day 6: Until We Meet Again!

After one last Goan breakfast, we head to the airport, our hearts full of memories, our spirits renewed, and our souls painted in the colours of Goa. Adeus, until next time!
